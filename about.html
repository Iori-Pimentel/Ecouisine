About Us

The purpose of this site is to offer foods, dishes, or meal preps that are affordable for students. On this site, aspiring cooks and people in general would also be able to view the recipes of the meals that are presented on the site. This site is created with the purpose of giving students the chance to eat and save money at the same time with its low-cost or budget-friendly meals.

Ecouisine is composed of dedicated students of St. Louise University under the course of Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, who are motivated to give people the chance to save money while eating at the same time.
